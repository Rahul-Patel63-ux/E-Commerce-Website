Book Recive

Returning books on time helps ensure everyone has fair access to library resources. It keeps the system running smoothly, allowing others to borrow and enjoy the books too. Late returns can result in fees and inconvenience for others waiting for the same book. Be considerate—return on time!

Food

Please refrain from bringing food or drinks into the library to maintain a clean and comfortable space for everyone. Spills and crumbs can damage books and equipment and may attract pests. Let's all work together to keep our library environment welcoming and in great condition!

Silance

Please use a quiet voice in the library to respect others who are reading, studying, or working. Keeping noise to a minimum helps everyone stay focused and enjoy a peaceful environment. Let’s work together to create a calm space for all!

Return books to their proper place

Please return books to their proper place after use to help maintain an organized and efficient library. When items are left out of place, it makes it harder for others to find what they need. Let’s all play a part in keeping our library tidy and accessible!
